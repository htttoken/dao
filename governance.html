Governance Structure

HTT DAO uses a transparent, collaborative governance model that reflects our focus on usability, reward redemption, and community participation — without staking or speculative voting power.

🧩 Core Roles

  • Contributors: Community members who help propose or review platform policies.
  • Vendors: Approved partners (hotels, transport, tours) who may vote on reward structures or platform updates.
  • Admin Council: Temporary steering group responsible for emergency actions, multisig approvals, and enforcing DAO rules.

📜 Proposal Types

  • ⚙️ Change reward percentage or affiliate payout
  • 🏨 Approve new vendor categories (e.g., “Spa Services”)
  • 🛡️ Suggest security rule updates (withdrawal limits, KYC tiers)
  • 📈 Add community incentives or remove features

📬 How Decisions Are Made

We currently use an off-chain voting mechanism:

  • Discussion Phase: Proposals are discussed via Telegram, Discord, or the community form.
  • Voting Phase: Snapshot or off-chain survey tools are used to collect votes (equal voting power).
  • Execution: Admins carry out approved proposals and publish actions on the Transparency page.

🧱 Future Upgrade Plan

  1. Phase 1: Manual Proposals with Admin Execution
  2. Phase 2: Snapshot Integration (2025 Q4)
  3. Phase 3: On-chain Voting via Smart Contract (optional, 2026+)
