Welcome to HTT DAO

HTT DAO (Decentralized Autonomous Organization) empowers users, vendors, and contributors of the HTT ecosystem to participate in decision-making processes, uphold transparency, and guide the evolution of the platform — without any speculation or staking.

Why We Built a DAO

  • ✅ Give HTT users and vendors a voice in key updates
  • ✅ Publish transparent reward reports and token actions
  • ✅ Decide on vendor approval rules, affiliate policies, and platform upgrades
  • ✅ Build trust without relying on token staking or speculation

How to Participate

Participation in HTT DAO is currently off-chain, and open to all registered contributors and verified partners. You can:

  1. View and respond to proposals
  2. Join the HTT Governance Group on Telegram/Discord
  3. Submit suggestions via the community page
