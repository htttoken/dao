HTT DAO Constitution

This constitution defines the purpose, scope, and foundational rules of HTT DAO — built to empower users, ensure reward transparency, and protect HTT's real-world use integrity.

Article 1 – Purpose

HTT DAO exists to govern key decisions related to the Hotel Travel Token ecosystem — including reward logic, platform rules, and utility expansion — through transparent, inclusive, and non-speculative processes.

Article 2 – Token Policy

  • HTT is a utility and reward-based token, not designed for speculation or staking.
  • Price indexing is based on internal service parity, not market demand.
  • Token burns, mints, and reward distributions must be publicly logged.

Article 3 – Proposal Eligibility

  • All verified contributors, travelers, or vendors may submit suggestions.
  • Formal proposals may only be initiated by approved DAO members or Admin Council.
  • Voting rights are currently equal-weighted and off-chain.

Article 4 – Scope of Governance

DAO decisions may include:

  • Affiliate reward logic and distribution ratios
  • Approved vendor participation models
  • Fee structures and wallet integration updates
  • Security or transparency policy upgrades

Note: DAO decisions may not interfere with contract-level functions unless an upgrade proposal is passed.

Article 5 – Anti-Speculation Principle

The HTT DAO explicitly prohibits manipulation, pump schemes, or speculative mechanisms within its governance or distribution models. All proposals that create volatility risks will be rejected.
